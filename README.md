# Arcade Game Project Brief

By: Sean Lin

## 1. Game Overview

### Original game selected:
The Sans boss fight from Undertale.

### Unique twist features:

#### [Full Of Determination]:
Players can choose to turn this ON or OFF. It prevents the player from dying (HP stays at 1), so they can practice the patterns as many times as they want.

#### False Ending:
I will design a Normal mode and a Hard mode. The Hard mode is hidden at first and only appears after the player finishes the Normal mode.

#### Target Audience:
Fans of Undertale and players who love challenging boss fights.

## 2. Gameplay Elements

Original Features:

### Soul Control :
2D movement of a “Soul” heart inside a restricted battle box.

### Special Attacks:
Blue attacks (don’t move to stay safe) and Orange attacks (must keep moving to stay safe).

### Gravity Physics:
Switching the Soul from Red mode (free move) to Blue mode (gravity jump).

## New Features/Twist Details:

### Dynamic Music:
Different music for each mode. The Hard mode music will be more intense to match the difficulty.

### Story Transition:
At the end of Normal mode, there will be a short plot scene that leads the player to unlock Hard mode.

### The Meta Ending:
When the player beats Hard mode, the boss will realize he is in a game and say that the author (Sean) will make him even stronger in the future.

## 3. Asset Requirements

### Graphics:
Sans (Boss): Normal, Sweating, and Glowing eyes versions.
Heart (Player): Red, Blue, and Cracked versions.
Attacks: Bones and Blasters in White, Orange, and Blue colours.

### Animations:
Sans breathing loop (idle animation).

Heart-shattering animation (for Game Over).

Screen shake effect (to make the blasters feel more powerful).

### UI (User Interface):

Main Menu with a locked Hard mode.

HP bar.

A toggle switch for [Full Of Determination].

### Source:
Sans, bones, blasters, and music will be sourced online. I will remove the backgrounds to make them transparent PNGs, so they fit the game.

### Sound:
#### Background Music:
Menu music, Megalovania (Normal), and Megalovania Hard Mode Remix (from YouTube).

#### Sound Effects:
Soul hit, the iconic “Ding” for Blue Soul mode, Blaster laser sound, and menu click sounds.
